About Us

A creator partnership agency, built for technology brands.

We're building MediaExpandFlow around a simple idea: technical products deserve creators who actually understand them.

Our Story

Why we exist

We started MediaExpandFlow because most influencer marketing agencies are built for consumer brands — fashion, beauty, lifestyle — and it shows. Technical products get generic outreach, mismatched creators, and campaigns that never quite land.

We're building a specialized creator partnership agency focused on one thing: helping AI, SaaS, fintech, and developer-facing companies connect with creators who can actually explain what they've built, to an audience that will care. That focus shapes everything — from who we contact, to how we scope a campaign, to what we consider a good fit.

We're early, and we're deliberately staying narrow rather than trying to serve every industry. We'd rather be excellent for technology brands than average for everyone.

How We Work

The principles behind every campaign

01

Focus over breadth

We work only with technology companies, so our understanding of your product and audience stays sharp.

02

One point of contact

Brands talk to us, not a rotating cast of creators. We handle the coordination underneath.

03

Transparency by default

You'll always know where a campaign stands — who's been contacted, what's agreed, what's next.

04

No inflated promises

We won't guarantee viral results. We commit to a rigorous, well-run process instead.

What We're Not

Clarity on what we do — and don't — do

We are not a social media management agency

We don't post on your channels or manage your social accounts. We manage relationships with outside creators.

We are not a general digital marketing agency

We don't run ads, SEO, or content marketing. Creator partnerships are the entirety of what we do.

Let's talk about your campaign

We're happy to walk through what a good fit looks like — no pressure, no obligation.
