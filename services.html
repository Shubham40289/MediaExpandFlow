Influencer Campaign Management

One service, done thoroughly. We run the full lifecycle of a creator campaign — from your first brief to the final published post.

1Creator Discovery

Finding creators who actually fit your product

We start by understanding your product, audience, and campaign goals in detail. From there, we search across platforms for creators whose content, tone, and audience genuinely align with what you're building — not just accounts with large followings.

What we evaluate

Audience relevance, content style, engagement quality, past brand mentions, and topical authority in your space.

2Creator Selection

A curated shortlist, not a long list

You review a small, considered set of options with clear reasoning for each recommendation.

Turning research into a short, considered list

Rather than handing you a spreadsheet of every possible name, we narrow the field to a shortlist of creators worth your time — with context on why each one was chosen. You approve who moves forward.

3Outreach

We make the first contact, professionally

Once you've approved a shortlist, we reach out to creators directly, presenting your brand and campaign clearly. This keeps the process moving without your team having to draft or send a single message.

Handled on your behalf

Every outreach message is sent, tracked, and followed up on by our team, under your brand's guidelines.

4Negotiation

Fair terms, clearly scoped

Rates, deliverables, usage rights, and timelines are agreed before any work begins.

Aligning rates and scope with your budget

We handle rate discussions and deliverable scope directly with creators, keeping terms fair for both sides and within the budget you've set. You're kept informed at every stage of the negotiation.

5Coordination

Keeping every moving part on schedule

Once terms are agreed, we manage briefs, content timelines, and approval rounds — making sure creators have what they need and deadlines are met without your team following up.

Fully tracked

Briefs, drafts, revisions, and approvals are managed and logged so nothing falls through the cracks.

6Campaign Management

From brief to publish

We stay involved through publish day, confirming deliverables match what was agreed.

Overseeing the campaign until it's fully delivered

We oversee the entire campaign through to completion — confirming content goes live as planned and every deliverable is fulfilled. You get a single, reliable point of contact for the whole engagement.

Have a campaign in mind?

Walk us through your goals on a discovery call and we'll map out what's realistic.
